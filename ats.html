Data Privacy & Retention

Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) — retention limits and right to erasure

Retention purge

Anonymises candidates who applied more than the retention window ago and were not hired. Name, email, phone, cover note and CV are removed; the record is kept (anonymous) so your reports and history stay accurate.

Right to erasure

A candidate may ask you to delete their data at any time. Enter their email to anonymise every application they submitted.
